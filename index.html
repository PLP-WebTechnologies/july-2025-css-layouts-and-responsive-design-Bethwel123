Our Story

Founded in 1998 by Njeri Wambui in Nairobi's bustling CBD, Mama Njeri's Kitchen started as a small kibanda serving construction workers. Word quickly spread about Njeri's delicious cooking, especially her signature nyama choma recipe passed down from her grandmother.

Today, under the management of Njeri's daughter Wanjiru, we've grown into a beloved Nairobi institution while maintaining the same authentic flavors and homely atmosphere.

"Good food is not just about taste, it's about love and tradition." - Mama Njeri
